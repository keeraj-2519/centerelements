About Nature

Nature is the ultimate source of our living. Both living and non-living things include nature, and everyone is interdependent, which helps maintain the ecosystem. Plants, animals, and humans all depend on nature for their survival.

watering the plants

What is watering the plants? Plants need water for survival and to grow. Plants use carbon dioxide along with water to make their food via photosynthesis. Water also helps in the transport of nutrients in plants. Seeds can not germinate without water.


Important Function of a Plant


Plants provide us with food, fiber, shelter, medicine, and fuel. The basic food for all organisms is produced by green plants. In the process of food production, oxygen is released. This oxygen, which we obtain from the air we breathe, is essential to life.
